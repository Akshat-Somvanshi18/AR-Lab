About Our Project

Welcome to our Augmented Reality (AR) project, a groundbreaking initiative that blends advanced technology with user-friendly design to transform how we interact with the world around us. By combining AR.js, A-Frame, and 3D modeling, our platform creates immersive experiences for learning, gaming, and visualizations.

The project focuses on making AR accessible, engaging, and impactful for diverse applications ranging from education to entertainment. Our interactive models and animations bring creativity and innovation to life.
